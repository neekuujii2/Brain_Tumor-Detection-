Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

How accurate is the brain tumor prediction based on MRI scans?
Our prediction model has been trained on a large dataset of MRI scans and has shown high accuracy in detecting brain tumors.
Is it safe to upload my MRI scan to the website?
Yes, we take data privacy and security very seriously. Your MRI scan will be encrypted and stored securely.
How long does it take to get the prediction results?
The prediction results are usually available within a few minutes after uploading your MRI scan.
Can the website detect all types of brain tumors?
Our prediction model is designed to detect the most common types of brain tumors. However, it may not be able to detect rare or complex tumors.
Can I use the prediction results as a substitute for a medical diagnosis?
No, the prediction results should not be used as a substitute for a medical diagnosis. It is always recommended to consult with a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis.
